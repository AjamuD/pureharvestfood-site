Program Requirements

We seek partners who share our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Our retailer program is designed for businesses that:

  • Operate established retail businesses (physical stores, online shops, or specialty food markets)
  • Demonstrate a commitment to premium food products and quality customer service
  • Have the capacity to meet minimum order requirements (starting at $300)
  • Provide appropriate storage and display conditions for premium food products
  • Share our values of authenticity, quality, and customer satisfaction
