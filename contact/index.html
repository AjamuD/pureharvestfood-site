Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products, orders, and services.

We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days) and express shipping (2-3 business days) within Trinidad and Tobago. International shipping is available with delivery times varying by destination. Free shipping is available on orders over $300.

Yes, we offer competitive wholesale pricing for bulk orders. Please contact our Sales department at pureharvest2022@gmail.com or call +1 868 361-8990 ext. 101 for custom pricing based on your order volume and requirements.

Our products are HACCP certified, GMP certified, and we maintain strict quality control standards. We also have Certificate of Origin documentation for international trade. All certifications are available upon request.

Our dehydrated products have a shelf life of 12-24 months when stored in a cool, dry place. Mixed nuts and seeds should be consumed within 6-12 months for optimal freshness. All products include expiration dates on packaging.

Yes, we offer facility tours by appointment. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to schedule your visit. Tours are available Monday through Friday during business hours and include our production areas and quality control processes.
