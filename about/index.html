Est. 2022

A Proudly Tobagonian Business

Pure Harvest Group of Companies Ltd. is a proudly Tobagonian-owned business dedicated to bringing the authentic flavors and traditions of the Caribbean to the world. Founded with a passion for natural, high-quality products, we specialize in the production and distribution of all-natural foods including dehydrated spices, mixed fruits, nuts, and seeds, caramel browning infusions, Accra mixes, spicy honey, and more.

Every product we create is carefully developed using traditional Caribbean ingredients and methods, reflecting the rich culinary heritage of Trinidad and Tobago. From our signature Nutri-Fuse snack line to our uniquely seasoned Caribbean browning blends, our mission is to offer products that are flavorful, wholesome, and made with integrity.

Our Home

Proudly Based in Moriah, Tobago

Headquartered in Moriah, Tobago, Pure Harvest is deeply connected to the rich cultural heritage and natural bounty of our island home. Our location in the lush hills of Tobago provides us with access to some of the freshest tropical ingredients and traditional knowledge.

Our facility combines traditional methods with modern food safety standards, allowing us to create products that honor our heritage while meeting the highest quality expectations.
