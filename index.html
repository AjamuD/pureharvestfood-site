Our Caribbean Heritage

Founded in 2022, Pure Harvest Group of Companies Ltd. is dedicated to bringing the authentic flavors of Trinidad and Tobago to kitchens around the world. Our journey began with a simple mission: to create all-natural, premium food products that celebrate our Caribbean heritage.

We carefully source the finest ingredients from local farmers, ensuring that every product meets our high standards for quality and taste. From our dehydrated spices to our signature Nutri-Fuse line, each item is crafted with care and attention to detail.
