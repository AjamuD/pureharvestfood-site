The Nutri-Fuse Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a beloved Caribbean brand, discover the journey behind our premium nut and seed blends

2020
The Beginning

The idea for Nutri-Fuse was born during the global pandemic when our founder, Sarah Williams, began experimenting with creating nutritious snacks using locally sourced ingredients from Trinidad and Tobago.

Starting with just a small home kitchen operation, Sarah developed the first Lightly Salted blend, focusing on highlighting the natural flavors of premium nuts and seeds.

2021
Expanding Flavors

As word spread about the quality of our nut blends, demand grew rapidly. We expanded our flavor offerings to include Seasoned and Buss-ah-nut varieties, incorporating traditional Caribbean spices and flavors.

We also established partnerships with local farmers to ensure a steady supply of high-quality ingredients while supporting the local economy.

2022
Official Launch

The Nutri-Fuse line was officially launched as part of the newly formed Pure Harvest Group of Companies Ltd. We opened our first dedicated production facility in Tobago, implementing strict quality control measures.

The Dark Chocolate variety was introduced and quickly became a customer favorite, offering a perfect balance of indulgence and nutrition.

2023
Regional Recognition

Nutri-Fuse gained recognition throughout the Caribbean region, winning the "Best New Food Product" award at the Caribbean Food Expo. We expanded distribution to major supermarkets and specialty food stores across Trinidad and Tobago.

The Red Skins variety was added to the lineup, featuring nuts with their natural skins intact for maximum nutritional benefit.

2024-Present
Growing Globally

Today, Nutri-Fuse products are available throughout the Caribbean and have begun expanding into international markets. We remain committed to our founding principles of quality, sustainability, and supporting local communities.

We continue to innovate with new flavors and packaging options while maintaining the premium quality that our customers have come to expect from the Nutri-Fuse name.

Flavor Profiles

Explore our range of delicious flavors, each crafted to deliver a unique and satisfying snacking experience

Lightly Salted

Customer Favorite

Our classic blend with just a touch of sea salt to enhance the natural flavors of premium nuts and seeds. Perfect for everyday snacking.

Key Ingredients:

Almonds, Cashews, Walnuts, Pumpkin Seeds, Sunflower Seeds, Sea Salt

Pairs Well With:

Fresh fruit, cheese platters, or enjoyed on its own as a protein-rich snack

Seasoned

Spice Lover's Choice

A savory blend featuring traditional Caribbean herbs and spices for a bold, flavorful snacking experience that captures the essence of island cuisine.

Key Ingredients:

Almonds, Cashews, Peanuts, Pumpkin Seeds, Caribbean Herb Blend, Sea Salt

Pairs Well With:

Cold beverages, as a topping for salads, or as part of a spicy trail mix

Buss-ah-nut

Bold & Spicy

A unique sweet and spicy blend that delivers a burst of flavor with every bite. Features a proprietary spice mix that will tantalize your taste buds.

Key Ingredients:

Cashews, Peanuts, Almonds, Honey, Caribbean Pepper Blend, Sea Salt

Pairs Well With:

Cold beer, as a party snack, or as a spicy addition to homemade trail mix

Dark Chocolate

Bestseller

A decadent blend featuring premium nuts coated in rich dark chocolate made from Trinidad and Tobago cocoa. The perfect balance of indulgence and nutrition.

Key Ingredients:

Almonds, Cashews, Dark Chocolate (70% cocoa), Cocoa Powder, Organic Cane Sugar

Pairs Well With:

Coffee, red wine, or as a dessert alternative that satisfies sweet cravings

Red Skins

Most Nutritious

Our most nutritious blend featuring nuts with their natural skins intact, preserving maximum antioxidant content and delivering a more robust flavor profile.

Key Ingredients:

Skin-on Almonds, Skin-on Peanuts, Walnuts, Pumpkin Seeds, Himalayan Pink Salt

Pairs Well With:

Green tea, as a topping for oatmeal, or blended into smoothies for added nutrition
