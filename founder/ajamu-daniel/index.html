A Journey of Passion and Purpose

Ajamu Daniel is a visionary entrepreneur from Tobago, Trinidad and Tobago, and the founder and Director of Pure Harvest Group of Companies Ltd. Since establishing the company in 2022, he has been dedicated to creating authentic, all-natural Tobago-made products that preserve the island's rich culinary traditions while meeting modern global standards.

Under his leadership, Pure Harvest has developed a diverse portfolio of products, including dehydrated spices, caramel browning, Nutri-Fuse mixed fruits and nuts, Accra mixes, and dehydrated fruit snacks — and many more on the horizon. His products are proudly featured on supermarket shelves across Tobago and Trinidad, with expanding reach into both regional and international markets.

Beyond business, Ajamu is passionate about innovation, product development, and supporting local agriculture. His happiest moment so far has been seeing Pure Harvest's products reach supermarket shelves and knowing they represent the flavors of Tobago to the world.

A graduate of Signal Hill Secondary School (Class of 1999), Ajamu enjoys cooking, Architecture, gardening, traveling, and exploring new business ideas. His biggest inspiration is his daughter, Ssanyu Zvi Daniel. Her curiosity, joy, and love motivate him daily to build a lasting, sustainable legacy.

He also thanks his life partner and fellow Director, Candice Joseph, who leads production at Pure Harvest, along with his colleagues at the Division of Settlements for their support in testing and refining the company's products.

Career Journey & Milestones

From vision to reality: The key moments that shaped Pure Harvest's success

The Beginning

2022

Founded Pure Harvest Group of Companies Ltd. with a vision to showcase authentic Tobago-made products. Launched the first line of Nutri-Fuse mixed fruits and nuts.

Building Foundations

2023

Expanded the Nutri-Fuse range. Secured supermarket placements across Tobago and Trinidad, building strong local brand recognition.

Product Line Expansion

2024

Introduced Caramel Browning (nutmeg, curry, bay leaf). Released Accra Mixes (saltfish, chicken, smoked fish). Strengthened supermarket presence and began exploring regional export opportunities.

Heritage & Innovation

2025

Secured supermarket placements across Tobago and Trinidad, creating a foundation for brand recognition.

Legacy Building

2026

Expanded distribution into wider Caribbean markets. Upgraded equipment and systems to increase production efficiency. Positioned Pure Harvest as a trusted Caribbean agro-processed brand.

International Reach

2027

Secured first international export deals in North America and Europe. Expanded product offerings to suit gourmet and specialty markets abroad. Scaled production to meet growing overseas demand.

Heritage & Innovation

2028

Launched premium product lines and advanced sustainable packaging initiatives. Strengthened Pure Harvest’s identity as both a cultural ambassador and innovator. Expanded e-commerce presence to serve global consumers directly

Legacy Building

2029

Established Pure Harvest as a recognized Caribbean household brand. Mentored and collaborated with local entrepreneurs to uplift Tobago’s agro-processing sector. Continued driving product innovation, ensuring growth and sustainability.
